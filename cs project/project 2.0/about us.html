Our History

Since our founding in 1998, we have been committed to providing our customers with the best possible car buying experience. Over the years, we have built a reputation for honesty, integrity, and outstanding customer service.

  • 1998 - Our dealership is founded by John Smith.
  • 2002 - We become an authorized Nissan GT-R dealer.
  • 2006 - We expand our inventory to include pre-owned vehicles.
  • 2012 - We open a new state-of-the-art service center.
  • 2020 - We celebrate 22 years of serving the community.
